About Our Application?

A chat application is a digital platform that allows users to communicate with each other in real-time or asynchronously.
Chat apps can be used to send text messages, images, digital files.
Chat apps are a popular way to connect with friends, colleagues, and customers. They can be used for a variety of purposes,
such as:
Instant messaging: Send and receive messages in real-time
Group chats: Communicate with multiple people at once
Sharing media: Send photos, videos, and other files

Why is our Application?

It helps users to seamlessly converse with each other, exchange images, videos, documents and files.
